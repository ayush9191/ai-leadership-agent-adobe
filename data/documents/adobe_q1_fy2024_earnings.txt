Adobe Inc. — Q1 FY2024 Earnings Summary
Quarter Ended: March 1, 2024
(Based on Adobe's publicly released Q1 FY2024 earnings press release)

==========================================================
Q1 FY2024 FINANCIAL RESULTS
==========================================================

Revenue: $5.18 billion (up 11% YoY from $4.66 billion in Q1 FY2023)
  - Digital Media segment revenue: $3.82 billion (up 12% YoY)
  - Digital Experience segment revenue: $1.29 billion (up 10% YoY)
  - Publishing and Advertising: $0.07 billion

GAAP Operating Income: $1.62 billion
Non-GAAP Operating Income: $2.46 billion
GAAP Net Income: $0.62 billion (includes $1.0 billion Figma termination fee impact)
Non-GAAP Net Income: $1.95 billion
GAAP Diluted EPS: $1.36 (impacted by Figma breakup fee)
Non-GAAP Diluted EPS: $4.27

Note: Q1 FY2024 GAAP results include the $1.0 billion Figma acquisition termination fee, which significantly reduced GAAP operating income and net income. Non-GAAP results exclude this charge.

==========================================================
DIGITAL MEDIA — Q1 PERFORMANCE
==========================================================

Digital Media segment revenue: $3.82 billion (up 12% year-over-year)
Digital Media net new ARR: $432 million in Q1 FY2024

Creative Cloud:
  - Creative Cloud ARR: $12.52 billion (end of Q1 FY2024)
  - Firefly cumulative generations surpassed 6.5 billion by end of Q1
  - Generative AI features rolled out in Photoshop, Illustrator, and Express
  - Adobe Express user growth accelerating with free AI-powered templates
  - Creative Cloud enterprise adoption growing in media and entertainment

Document Cloud:
  - Document Cloud ARR: $3.09 billion (end of Q1 FY2024)
  - Acrobat AI Assistant launched in beta — early enterprise interest strong
  - PDF productivity and collaboration features driving mobile usage
  - Acrobat on web growing as free tier draws users into conversion funnel

==========================================================
DIGITAL EXPERIENCE — Q1 PERFORMANCE
==========================================================

Digital Experience segment revenue: $1.29 billion (up 10% YoY)
Digital Experience subscription revenue: $1.16 billion (up 12% YoY)

Key highlights:
  - Adobe Experience Platform enhancements announced at Adobe Summit 2024
  - Real-Time CDP gaining enterprise momentum in financial services and healthcare
  - Content Supply Chain solution positioning resonating with CMOs
  - AEM Edge Delivery Services expanding for fast, developer-friendly web experiences
  - Journey Optimizer adoption accelerating for omnichannel customer engagement

==========================================================
FIGMA TERMINATION IMPACT
==========================================================

  - Adobe and Figma mutually terminated the proposed acquisition on December 17, 2023
  - Adobe paid a $1.0 billion termination fee to Figma in Q1 FY2024
  - Termination followed regulatory challenges from EU Commission and UK CMA
  - Management stated Adobe would pursue organic innovation in web-based collaborative design
  - XD development was deemphasized; resources redirected to Express and Creative Cloud web apps

==========================================================
CASH FLOW AND CAPITAL ALLOCATION — Q1
==========================================================

Operating Cash Flow: $2.12 billion in Q1 FY2024
Share Repurchases: $2.0 billion in Q1 FY2024
Cash and Short-Term Investments: $7.14 billion (end of Q1)
Total Debt: $3.65 billion (investment-grade rated)

==========================================================
FY2024 GUIDANCE (ISSUED WITH Q1 RESULTS)
==========================================================

Revenue: approximately $21.30–$21.50 billion (representing ~10-11% growth)
Digital Media net new ARR: approximately $1.90 billion
Non-GAAP EPS: approximately $18.00–$18.20
Effective Non-GAAP tax rate: approximately 18.5%

==========================================================
NOTABLE RISKS IN Q1
==========================================================

  - Figma termination fee reduced GAAP earnings significantly — one-time event
  - Regulatory environment remains challenging: EU Digital Markets Act, AI Act compliance ahead
  - Subscription cancellation practices FTC scrutiny — lawsuit filed June 2024
  - Free AI tools (Canva, free Midjourney tier) putting pricing pressure on entry-level creative users
  - SMB segment conversion rates showing sensitivity to price increases
  - China market continued softness due to domestic alternatives and economic slowdown
