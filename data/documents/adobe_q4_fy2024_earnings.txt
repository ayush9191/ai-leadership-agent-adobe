Adobe Inc. — Q4 FY2024 Earnings Summary
Quarter Ended: November 29, 2024
(Based on Adobe's publicly released Q4 FY2024 earnings press release)

==========================================================
Q4 FY2024 FINANCIAL RESULTS
==========================================================

Revenue: $5.61 billion (up 11% YoY from $5.05 billion in Q4 FY2023)
  - Digital Media segment revenue: $4.15 billion (up 12% YoY)
  - Digital Experience segment revenue: $1.40 billion (up 10% YoY)
  - Publishing and Advertising: $0.06 billion

GAAP Operating Income: $2.00 billion
Non-GAAP Operating Income: $2.73 billion
GAAP Net Income: $1.68 billion
Non-GAAP Net Income: $2.31 billion
GAAP Diluted EPS: $3.79
Non-GAAP Diluted EPS: $5.23

==========================================================
DIGITAL MEDIA — Q4 PERFORMANCE
==========================================================

Digital Media segment revenue: $4.15 billion (up 12% year-over-year)
Digital Media net new ARR: $578 million in Q4 FY2024

Creative Cloud:
  - Creative Cloud ARR: $13.55 billion (end of Q4 FY2024)
  - Strong adoption of Firefly-powered features across Photoshop, Illustrator, and Express
  - Over 16 billion cumulative Firefly generations since launch
  - Adobe Express continued rapid growth with Firefly integration
  - GenStudio for Performance Marketing gaining enterprise traction

Document Cloud:
  - Document Cloud ARR: $3.47 billion (end of Q4 FY2024)
  - Acrobat AI Assistant driving engagement and conversion
  - Growing adoption of Reader across web and mobile platforms
  - Strong enterprise demand for document workflows with AI capabilities

==========================================================
DIGITAL EXPERIENCE — Q4 PERFORMANCE
==========================================================

Digital Experience segment revenue: $1.40 billion (up 10% YoY)
Digital Experience subscription revenue: $1.28 billion (up 12% YoY)

Key highlights:
  - Adobe Experience Platform adoption continued to grow
  - Real-Time CDP expanding enterprise customer base
  - Journey Optimizer showing strong growth in omnichannel orchestration
  - Adobe Commerce maintaining position despite competitive e-commerce landscape
  - AI capabilities in Experience Platform driving deeper platform engagement

==========================================================
CASH FLOW AND BALANCE SHEET — Q4
==========================================================

Operating Cash Flow (Q4): $2.87 billion
Cash, cash equivalents, and short-term investments: $7.89 billion
Total debt: $5.65 billion
Share repurchases in Q4: Approximately $3.0 billion

==========================================================
MANAGEMENT COMMENTARY
==========================================================

CEO Shantanu Narayen highlighted:
  - "Adobe delivered a strong finish to fiscal 2024, achieving record revenue driven by the strength of our Creative Cloud, Document Cloud, and Experience Cloud offerings."
  - Firefly's rapid adoption demonstrates the value of commercially safe generative AI
  - The company is well-positioned for the AI opportunity across all three clouds
  - Continued investment in AI innovation remains the top priority

CFO Dan Durn noted:
  - Strong revenue growth driven by Digital Media and Digital Experience
  - Healthy operating margins reflect operational discipline combined with strategic investment
  - Cash flow generation remains robust, supporting share repurchase program
  - FY2025 guidance reflects confidence in sustained demand across product portfolio
