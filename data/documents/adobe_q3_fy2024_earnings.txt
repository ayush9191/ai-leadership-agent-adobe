Adobe Inc. — Q3 FY2024 Earnings Summary
Quarter Ended: August 30, 2024
(Based on Adobe's publicly released Q3 FY2024 earnings press release)

==========================================================
Q3 FY2024 FINANCIAL RESULTS
==========================================================

Revenue: $5.41 billion (up 11% YoY from $4.89 billion in Q3 FY2023)
  - Digital Media segment revenue: $3.99 billion (up 12% YoY)
  - Digital Experience segment revenue: $1.35 billion (up 10% YoY)
  - Publishing and Advertising: $0.07 billion

GAAP Operating Income: $1.90 billion
Non-GAAP Operating Income: $2.60 billion
GAAP Net Income: $1.48 billion
Non-GAAP Net Income: $2.09 billion
GAAP Diluted EPS: $3.26
Non-GAAP Diluted EPS: $4.65

==========================================================
DIGITAL MEDIA — Q3 PERFORMANCE
==========================================================

Digital Media segment revenue: $3.99 billion (up 12% year-over-year)
Digital Media net new ARR: $504 million in Q3 FY2024

Creative Cloud:
  - Creative Cloud ARR: $13.26 billion (end of Q3 FY2024)
  - Photoshop on the web and mobile continued to gain users
  - Firefly Image Model 3 released with photorealistic quality improvements
  - Firefly Vector Model introduced in Illustrator for AI-native vector generation
  - Premiere Pro audio enhancement and AI editing features gaining adoption
  - Adobe MAX 2024 announcements expected to drive further innovation

Document Cloud:
  - Document Cloud ARR: $3.34 billion (end of Q3 FY2024)
  - Acrobat AI Assistant expanding across enterprise accounts
  - Liquid Mode and AI-powered summarization driving mobile engagement

==========================================================
DIGITAL EXPERIENCE — Q3 PERFORMANCE
==========================================================

Digital Experience segment revenue: $1.35 billion (up 10% YoY)
Digital Experience subscription revenue: $1.23 billion (up 12% YoY)

Key highlights:
  - Adobe Experience Platform continued as foundation for enterprise digital transformation
  - Real-Time CDP and Journey Optimizer showing strong enterprise pipeline
  - Adobe Summit announcements driving awareness and pipeline for Experience Cloud
  - AI Assistant capabilities expanding across Experience Platform

==========================================================
NOTABLE RISKS IN Q3
==========================================================

  - Enterprise deal cycles remain elongated in certain regions due to macroeconomic caution
  - Foreign exchange headwinds continued to impact reported revenue — estimated ~2% negative FX impact
  - Competitive landscape intensifying with Canva's enterprise push and Figma's product expansion
  - Midjourney and other AI image generators creating pricing pressure in creative tools
  - China market softness persisting due to local competition and economic conditions

==========================================================
MANAGEMENT OUTLOOK — Q3
==========================================================

  - Adobe raised full-year revenue guidance slightly following Q3 results
  - Management expressed confidence in Digital Media net new ARR trajectory
  - Continued emphasis on AI-driven product innovation across all three clouds
  - Q4 FY2024 expected to benefit from Adobe MAX product launches and holiday-driven demand
